My photography setup

My current photography setup is the Fujifiilm X-T30 and the Tokina 33mm f1.4 lens that I bought from a used lens store in Japan. Even though it's not the newest and most "high tech" setup, it is probably the most perfect setup I could ask for. Crispy sharp images, beautiful colors, and small enough to take everywhere I go. The thing I love most about this camera however, are the film recipes built into the camera. It allows me to esentially edit the images before I even shoot! I hardly ever shoot in raw anymore unless it's for clients, for me it's more fun like this and it adds a certain satisfation when you get a perfect image straight from the camera.
